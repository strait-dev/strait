description: "Frequently asked questions about Strait pricing, billing, and plan limits."
icon: "circle-question"
---

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Can I try Strait for free?">
    Yes. The Free plan includes 5,000 orchestration runs per month, 3 concurrent runs, 10 workflow steps, and 7-day run history. No credit card is required.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What is an orchestration run?">
    An orchestration run is customer work dispatched by Strait. It includes job runs and workflow step runs. Automatic system retries do not add runs. Manual replays do.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How does billing work?">
    Starter, Pro, Scale, and Business can be billed monthly or annually. Annual plans are billed up front for 12 months. Enterprise is quoted by contract.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What happens if I exceed my included monthly runs?">
    Free without a card is hard-capped at 5,000 orchestration runs per month. Paid plans have overage enabled by default and use the plan's default spending cap unless you change it in billing settings.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I disable overage?">
    Yes. With overage disabled, new runs suspend at the included allowance and schedules pause until the next billing period or until overage is re-enabled.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What happens when I reach my spending cap?">
    Strait sends a warning at 80 percent and suspends new dispatch at 100 percent. In-flight runs are allowed to finish. Suspension lifts when the cap is raised above current overage spend, the cap action is changed to notify-only, or the next billing period starts. The billing webhook events are `billing.cap_warning` and `billing.cap_reached`.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What is the SSO launch status?">
    No. SSO/SAML, SCIM, IP allowlisting, static IPs, VPC peering, data residency, single-tenant orchestration, and BYO-cloud are roadmap or contact-sales items for launch.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I self-host instead?">
    Yes. The community edition has no cloud billing limits. Self-hosting requires PostgreSQL, Redis, and Sequin. The cloud dashboard, vendor support, hosted cloud orchestration, and BYO-cloud contracts are not included.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What payment methods do you accept?">
    Self-serve plans use credit card payments through Stripe. Enterprise supports contract terms and invoice billing.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
