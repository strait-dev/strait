## What Happens at the Limit

When you reach your daily run limit, new runs are queued and processed the next day. You are never charged overage fees on Free or Starter plans.

On the Pro plan, contact us for burst pricing if you regularly exceed your daily limit.

Concurrent run limits are hard caps — if all slots are occupied, new runs wait in the queue until a slot opens.

## Self-Hosted

Strait is open-source under the Apache 2.0 license. Self-host on your infrastructure with no license fees, no run limits, and no feature restrictions.

**You provide:** PostgreSQL, Redis, and a server to run the Strait binary.

**You manage:** Updates, backups, monitoring, and scaling.

**You get:** Full control over your data and infrastructure. Every feature available in the cloud plans (except billing and managed multi-region) works in self-hosted mode.

See [SELFHOST.md](https://github.com/strait-dev/strait/blob/master/SELFHOST.md) for the setup guide.
