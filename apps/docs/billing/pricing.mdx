## Plans

| Feature | Free | Starter | Pro | Enterprise |
|---------|------|---------|-----|------------|
| Price | $0/mo | $29/mo | $99/mo | Custom |
| Organizations | 1 | 3 | 10 | Unlimited |
| Projects per org | 2 | 10 | 50 | Unlimited |
| Members per org | 3 | 10 | 25 | Unlimited |
| Runs per day | 5,000 | 50,000 | 500,000 | Unlimited |
| Concurrent runs | 5 | 25 | 100 | Custom |
| Retention | 1 day | 7 days | 30 days | Custom |
| RBAC | None | Basic | Full | Full |
| Audit logs | No | No | Yes | Yes |
| SSO/SAML | No | No | No | Yes |

All plans include every core feature: jobs, workflows, retries, event triggers, webhooks, and API access.

## Self-Hosted

Strait is open-source. You can self-host on your own infrastructure with PostgreSQL at no cost. The managed platform at [app.strait.dev](https://app.strait.dev) provides hosted infrastructure, automatic updates, and support.
