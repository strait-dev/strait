# Pricing

## Pricing Philosophy

Strait is built around three principles:

- **All core features are free on every plan.** Job orchestration, workflow DAGs, retries, event triggers, webhooks, and observability are available from day one—not locked behind paywalls.
- **Scale and governance are gated, not capabilities.** Plan limits exist to protect shared infrastructure and enable team-level access controls. You will never hit a wall where a feature simply does not exist on your plan.
- **Compute credit equals subscription price.** Every paid plan includes a monthly compute credit equal to its subscription cost. A $49/month Starter plan comes with $49 in compute credits—your subscription does not cost extra on top of compute.

## Compute Cost Breakdown

Managed execution (running jobs inside ephemeral Fly Machines) is billed at per-second rates based on the machine preset. HTTP execution against your own endpoints is not billed by Strait.

| Preset | vCPUs | Memory | Rate/sec | Example: 30s run |
|--------|-------|--------|----------|-----------------|
| micro | 1 | 256 MB | $0.017 | $0.51 |
| small | 1 | 512 MB | $0.034 | $1.02 |
| medium | 2 | 4 GB | $0.085 | $2.55 |
| large | 4 | 8 GB | $0.170 | $5.10 |
| large-2x | 16 | 32 GB | $0.680 | $20.40 |

Costs are calculated from `started_at` to `finished_at` at microsecond precision. You are never charged for time spent queued or waiting for a machine to provision.

<Callout type="info">
Compute credits included in your plan are consumed first. When credits are exhausted, usage is billed at the per-second rates above until the daily spending limit is reached.
</Callout>

## Overage Pricing

When your daily run limit is exceeded, additional runs are billed at:

- **$0.20 per 1,000 runs** beyond the daily limit

Compute usage above your included credit is billed at the per-second rates listed in the table above. Overages are calculated daily and invoiced at the end of your billing period.

You can set a **spending limit** to cap total charges. When the limit is reached, new runs are rejected until the next billing period or you raise the limit. See the [Billing FAQ](/docs/billing/faq) for details on spending limits.

## What Is Not Billed

- HTTP execution against your own endpoints (your infrastructure costs apply)
- Queued time before execution
- API calls to the Strait management API
- Webhook delivery attempts
- Log storage within your plan's retention window

## Pricing Transparency

All pricing is published publicly. There are no hidden fees, minimum commitments (except Enterprise), or usage-based price escalations outside the rates listed above. You can estimate costs for managed execution workloads using the table above before running a single job.
